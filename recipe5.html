Simple Chocolate Mug Cake

Preparation Time: 5 Minutes

Difficulty Level: Easy

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons All-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons Cocoa powder
  • 3 tablespoons Sugar
  • 3 tablespoons Milk
  • 1 tablespoon Vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. In a microwave-safe mug, mix the flour, cocoa powder, and sugar.
  2. Stir in the milk and vegetable oil until the batter is smooth.
  3. Microwave on high for 1 minute and 30 seconds.
  4. Let it cool for a minute before eating. Enjoy!
