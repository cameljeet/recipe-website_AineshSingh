Simple Garlic Pasta

Preparation Time: 15 Minutes

Difficulty Level: Easy

Ingredients

  • 200g Pasta
  • 2 tablespoons Olive Oil
  • 3 cloves Garlic, chopped
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil water in a pot and add the pasta. Cook until soft.
  2. In a pan, heat the olive oil and add the chopped garlic.
  3. Drain the pasta and add it to the pan with the garlic.
  4. Add salt and pepper, mix well, and serve hot!
