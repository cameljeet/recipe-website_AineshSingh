Creamy Tomato Soup

Preparation Time: 30 Minutes

Difficulty Level: Medium

Ingredients

  • 5 large Tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 Onion, diced
  • 2 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 cup Vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup Heavy cream (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook the diced onion and minced garlic in a pot until soft.
  2. Add the chopped tomatoes and vegetable broth. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  3. Blend the mixture until smooth.
  4. Stir in the heavy cream, heat gently, and serve with bread.
