Classic Veggie Burger

Preparation Time: 25 Minutes

Difficulty Level: Medium

Ingredients

  • 1 Veggie patty (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 Burger bun
  • Lettuce and Tomato slices
  • 1 slice Cheese (optional)
  • Ketchup and Mustard

Instructions

  1. Cook the veggie patty in a pan or on a grill until browned on both sides.
  2. Toast the burger bun lightly.
  3. Place the patty on the bottom bun and add the cheese slice to melt.
  4. Top with lettuce, tomato, ketchup, and mustard. Add the top bun and serve.
