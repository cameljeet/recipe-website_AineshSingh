Fresh Summer Salad

Preparation Time: 10 Minutes

Difficulty Level: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Mixed greens (lettuce, spinach)
  • 1 Cucumber, sliced
  • 1 cup Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 2 tablespoons Balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Wash all the vegetables thoroughly.
  2. Chop the cucumber and halve the cherry tomatoes.
  3. In a large bowl, mix the greens, cucumber, and tomatoes.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar, toss lightly, and serve.
